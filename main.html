Kyoto, Japan


Kyoto, once the capital of Japan, is a city on the island of Honshu known for its classical Buddhist temples, gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines, and traditional wooden houses. It offers a perfect blend of culture, nature, and tranquility.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Kinkaku-ji - The famous Golden Pavilion temple surrounded by a serene pond.
  2. Fushimi Inari Shrine - Walk through thousands of iconic red torii gates.
  3. Arashiyama Bamboo Grove - A mystical bamboo forest walk like no other.
  4. Gion District - Explore Kyoto’s historic district and maybe spot a Geisha.
  5. Philosopher’s Path - A peaceful canal-side walk especially beautiful during cherry blossom season.

Must-Try Foods

  • Sushi - Especially Kyoto-style pressed sushi.
  • Kaiseki - A traditional multi-course Japanese dinner.
  • Matcha desserts - Try everything from matcha ice cream to cakes.
  • Yudofu - A healthy and warm tofu dish served near temples.
  • Tempura - Lightly battered and perfectly fried vegetables and seafood.
